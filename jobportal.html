Job Portal Website

An online platform connecting job seekers with employers for a seamless recruitment experience

Introduction

This project is an online job portal that allows job seekers to browse job listings, apply for positions, and manage their career profiles. Employers can post job openings, review applicants, and manage recruitment. The platform aims to simplify the hiring process for both employers and candidates, offering an intuitive and efficient interface.

Objectives

  • Provide a user-friendly platform for job seekers to find and apply for job opportunities.
  • Allow employers to post job openings and manage the hiring process.
  • Enable job seekers to build and manage their resumes or portfolios online.
  • Offer job recommendations and personalized career advice based on user profiles.

Project Functionality

For Job Seekers:

  • Job Browsing: Search for jobs by role, location, salary, and company.
  • Resume Creation: Build and manage a digital resume with work experience, skills, and education.
  • Job Applications: Apply for jobs directly through the portal with one-click applications.
  • Job Alerts: Receive notifications for relevant job openings based on profile preferences.
  • Application Tracking: Track the status of job applications and interview schedules.
  • Profile Management: Update and maintain career profiles with personal information, skills, and achievements.

For Employers:

  • Job Posting: Create and manage job listings with detailed descriptions, qualifications, and requirements.
  • Candidate Screening: Review resumes, cover letters, and job applications from candidates.
  • Applicant Tracking: Track and manage the progress of job applicants through various hiring stages.
  • Job Dashboard: View insights, such as the number of applicants, application statuses, and overall recruitment progress.
  • Company Profile: Create and maintain an employer profile to showcase company culture and job openings.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: React.js / Angular / Vue.js
  • Backend: Node.js (Express) / Python (Django or Flask)
  • Database: MySQL / MongoDB / PostgreSQL
  • Authentication: OAuth, JWT for secure user login and registration
  • Search Functionality: Elasticsearch or Algolia for job listings and candidate searches
  • File Upload: AWS S3 or similar for managing resumes and cover letters
  • Admin Dashboard: React or Vue.js for creating the admin panel to manage users, job postings, and analytics
