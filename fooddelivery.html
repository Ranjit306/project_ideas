Food Delivery System

A platform for ordering food online and having it delivered to your doorstep quickly and efficiently

Introduction

The Food Delivery System is an online platform that allows users to order food from various restaurants and have it delivered to their location. The system enables users to browse restaurant menus, select dishes, make payments, and track the delivery status in real-time. It helps restaurants manage orders and deliveries efficiently while providing a smooth experience for customers.

Objectives

  • Provide a convenient platform for customers to order food from a wide range of restaurants.
  • Offer real-time order tracking, ensuring customers know the status of their deliveries.
  • Allow customers to make secure payments online through various payment methods.
  • Enable restaurants to manage orders, update menus, and track deliveries seamlessly.
  • Provide delivery drivers with an easy-to-use interface for managing deliveries and route planning.

Project Functionality

For Customers:

  • Restaurant Listings: Browse a list of available restaurants and food options based on location, cuisine, or rating.
  • Menu Browsing: View restaurant menus, including food items, prices, and descriptions.
  • Order Placement: Add food items to the cart, customize orders (if necessary), and proceed to checkout.
  • Secure Payments: Pay for orders using credit/debit cards, UPI, wallets, or cash on delivery.
  • Order Tracking: Track the status of the order, from kitchen preparation to delivery, in real-time.
  • Delivery Options: Choose delivery preferences such as scheduled delivery or immediate delivery.
  • Order History: View previous orders and reorder favorite meals with ease.

For Restaurants:

  • Menu Management: Add, update, or remove menu items, including prices and descriptions.
  • Order Management: View incoming orders, confirm, reject, or modify them, and track order preparation.
  • Payment Processing: Manage order payments and track earnings from deliveries.
  • Delivery Tracking: Coordinate with delivery drivers to ensure timely and accurate deliveries.
  • Customer Feedback: Receive ratings and reviews from customers to improve service and food quality.

For Delivery Drivers:

  • Order Assignment: Receive assigned orders and customer information for efficient deliveries.
  • Route Planning: View optimized routes for delivering orders based on real-time traffic and location.
  • Delivery Status Updates: Update the status of deliveries (picked up, on the way, delivered) and keep customers informed.
  • Driver Profile: Maintain a profile with delivery history, ratings, and feedback.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: React.js / Angular / Vue.js for the customer and restaurant interfaces
  • Backend: Node.js (Express) / Python (Django or Flask) for server-side management
  • Database: MySQL / PostgreSQL / MongoDB for managing customer orders, restaurant menus, and payment transactions
  • Authentication: OAuth, JWT for secure login and session management
  • Payment Gateway: Stripe / Razorpay / PayPal for processing payments securely
  • Real-Time Updates: WebSockets for live order tracking, delivery status updates, and notifications
  • Admin Dashboard: React or Vue.js to build an intuitive admin interface for managing orders, restaurants, and deliveries
  • Geolocation & Maps: Google Maps API for location tracking and optimized delivery routes
  • Cloud Hosting: AWS / Google Cloud for scalable hosting of the application
